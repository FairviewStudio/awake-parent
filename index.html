Built by a Parent,
For Parents.

"I created this app not as an expert, but as a parent who was tired of feeling lost in the storm."

Awake Parent was born from my own struggle to remain present during the chaos of daily life. It was only after discovering the profound wisdom in Dr. Shefali’s "The Awakened Family" that I realized my child wasn't a problem to be fixed, but a mirror reflecting my own unmet needs.

I built this app to translate those high-level spiritual concepts into practical, in-the-moment tools. It is my sanctuary, and I hope it becomes yours too—a place where we can heal our own triggers so we can finally see our children for who they truly are.
